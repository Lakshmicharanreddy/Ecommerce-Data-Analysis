# E-Commerce Performance Analysis

## Project Overview
This project analyzes an E-commerce dataset to evaluate business performance, 
identify growth opportunities, and provide strategic recommendations.

The analysis was performed using SQL (PostgreSQL) and Python (Pandas, Matplotlib).

---

## Business Objective
- Analyze overall revenue and profit trends
- Identify top-performing categories
- Evaluate marketing effectiveness (ROAS)
- Analyze regional sales distribution
- Provide actionable business recommendations

---

## Tools & Technologies Used
- PostgreSQL (SQL Queries)
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Jupyter Notebook
- pgAdmin

---

## Key KPIs Analyzed
- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin
- Year-over-Year Growth
- Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)
- Category-wise Performance
- Regional Revenue Distribution

---

## Key Insights

- Revenue peaked in 2022 and showed a decline in 2023.
- Home Decor generated the highest revenue contribution.
- Beauty category showed strong marketing efficiency (high ROAS).
- Books category underperformed despite marketing spend.
- North region contributed the highest overall sales.

---

##  Strategic Recommendations

- Increase marketing budget for high-ROAS categories.
- Optimize or reduce ad spend in low-performing segments.
- Focus expansion efforts in high-revenue regions.
- Improve pricing or bundling strategies in weaker categories.
